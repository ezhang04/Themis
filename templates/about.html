About Themis Bias Detector

Themis Bias Detector is a tool designed to analyze news articles for potential bias. By leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms and AI models, Themis evaluates the content of articles and provides a weighted bias score to help users identify biased reporting.

Features

  • Analyze articles by simply entering their URL.
  • Receive a detailed breakdown of bias indicators.
  • Get a weighted bias score to assess the level of bias.

How It Works

Themis the text of news articles once the user enters an article URL. It looks for specific linguistic patterns, sentiment, and framing that may indicate bias. The text is passed into 2 AI models, and using a weighted algorithm - return a confidence score on how biased / unbiased the article is. The results are then compiled into an easy-to-understand format for users.
