About Themis Bias Detector

Themis Bias Detector is a tool designed to analyze news articles for potential bias. By leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms and AI models, Themis evaluates article content and provides a weighted bias score to help users identify biased reporting.

Features

  • Analyze articles by simply entering their URL.
  • Receive a detailed breakdown of bias indicators.
  • Get a weighted bias score to assess the level of bias.

How It Works

Themis processes the text of news articles once a user enters a URL. It examines linguistic patterns, sentiment, and framing that may indicate bias. The text is passed into two AI models (one we trained ourselves and one that is pretrained) and, using a weighted algorithm, returns a confidence score indicating how biased or unbiased the article is. The results are then compiled into an easy-to-understand format for users.
